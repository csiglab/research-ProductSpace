Market Evolution Trajectory

Technology Trajectory

Migration from P-type PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) to N-type TOPCon (Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact) and HJT (Heterojunction Technology) architectures. Critical inflection point expected 2025-2026 as TOPCon achieves cost parity with PERC while offering 1.5-2% absolute efficiency gains.
